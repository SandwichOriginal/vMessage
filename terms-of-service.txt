2. WHAT THE SERVICE IS
----------------------------------------------------------------
vMessage is a browser-based, peer-to-peer chat application.
There is no central server that processes, stores, or relays
your messages. All communication happens directly between
users' browsers via WebRTC data channels.

The Service relies on the PeerJS public signaling server
(peerjs.com) solely to establish initial connections between
peers. After a connection is established, the signaling server
is no longer involved.
